Since 2009! Building Our Empire.

Team RGIT Racing has developed vehicles for SAE Supra and Formula Bharat competitions. In recent years, the team has provided students at MCT's Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology with hands-on experience in the design and manufacturing of performance cars. Student alumni members have now become successful professionals working and studying around the world. Before switching to Formula Bharat in 2019, we competed in multiple Supra SAE events. Our experience in combustion is comprehensible. However, the future is electric, and it is here now. With the 2022 season, we've finally jumped into EV territory. Our goal is to improve performance every year as we grow the team and car.
